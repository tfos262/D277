Fort Wayne

City of Fort Wayne

Fort Wayne is the second largest city by population in Indiana. It is home to 575,109 people (STATS Indiana, 2024). The city was founded in 1794 in the midst of America's Revolutionary War (Historic Fort Wayne, 2024). Fort Wayne is an urban city located in Northeastern Indiana. The average income per individual in Fort Wayne is $55,729, which is lower than the rest of Indiana at $58,323 (STATS Indiana, 2024).
