Carmel

City of Carmel

Carmel is home to a population of 101,964 Hoosiers (U.S. Census Bureau, 2024). The city of Carmel was officially founded in 1874, although the original name of the Town was Bethlehem (Haines, 2024). With over 150 roundabouts, Carmel has the most roundabouts of any US city (GRANICUS, 2024). The city is suburban and is located North Central of the State, as a Northern suburb of Indianapolis. The average income per household in Carmel is $132,859 (U.S. Census Bureau, 2024), which is higher than the rest of Indiana at $66,768 (STATS Indiana).
